Awodi

Awodi is Micro Aerial Vehicle made with a PCB frame, having a combination of an on - board position sensor and an Arduino for implementing and testing control strategies. Designed and constructed by @jimiolaniyan and @demukpere, it is a modification of this instructable by Shane Colton.

This drone also has a voice-activation feature whereby the drone can be commanded through voice inputs to an android device that are sent over a wireless network using xbees.

Poster Presentation

The abstract for this work was accepted for the 2016 University of Lagos Research Conference and Fair and we gave a poster presentation at the conference. See details here

Extending Capabilities

Future modifications are targetted at modifying the platform to become autonomous by adding a camera for vision and navigation.
